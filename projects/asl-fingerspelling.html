ASL Fingerspelling → Text (Real‑time)

Real-time American Sign Language (ASL) fingerspelling recognition that streams webcam video, extracts landmarks with MediaPipe, and decodes character sequences with a Transformer encoder–decoder.

PythonMediaPipePyTorchComputer Vision

Highlights:

  • Landmarks (pose + both hands) → strict 144‑feature vector per frame.
  • Robust normalization and interpolation without dropping samples.
  • Transformer encoder–decoder with temporal smoothing for stable streaming text.
  • Validation CER ≈ 0.36 in ~3.5 hours on 2× T4.
